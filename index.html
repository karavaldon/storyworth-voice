Do you remember the moment you became a dad?

Oh, yes. I remember thinking — I have no idea what I'm doing. But I also just knew I'd do anything for her. They put her in my arms and I just looked down at her and thought: well, that's it. That's my whole life right there.

I'd always heard people say your whole life changes. I thought I understood what that meant. I didn't. You can't, until someone puts a baby in your arms and she opens her eyes and looks at you like you're the only thing in the world.
